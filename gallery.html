Biography of the Headmaster

Name : Simphiwe
Surname: Kuze
Place of Birth : Dimbaza EC
Current City : Cape Town
Employment : Principal of Mzamomtsha Primary School

Education

• Mr Kuze attended Archie Velile Senior Secondary School in Dimbaza in the rural area in Eastern Cape.,
• He then came to Cape Town during the apartheid Era to pursue a career in Education and studied at the University of Western Cape (UWC), Where he did Bachelor of Arts(B.A) History and Creative Arts, because he love creativity and the fine of arts.
• He then further his education by doing a Higher Diploma in Education at the University of Western Cape(UWC)
• He completed his studies by doing Bachelor of Education and achieve his certificate in Education Management.

Work Experience

• Teacher at Mfuleni Combined School from March 1994 – April 1998.
• In April 1998 he started teaching at Manzomthombo Senior Secondary School until December 2000.
• In January 2002 he got a promotion from the school to be the Head of Department(HOD) at Manzomthombo SSS until January 2004.
• He was then promoted again to be the Deputy Principal of Manzomthombo SSS from 2004 November to January 2017.
• Because of his excellence in History and the love of teaching history he got a position to be a History National Senior certificate(NSC) Marker, Chief Marker and Internal Moderator he got this achievement in November 2001 until January 2017.
• In December 2017 he unfortunately resign from Manzomthombo SSS because of he got a higher position to become a Principal at Mzamomtsha Primary School he started with this position in January 2018 until Today he is the principal of the school.
